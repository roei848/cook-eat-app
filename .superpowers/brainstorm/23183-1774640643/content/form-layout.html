A. Multi-Step Wizard

3 focused steps with progress bar:
Step 1: Title, description, category, difficulty, time
Step 2: Ingredients list
Step 3: Preparation steps + photo

Pros

  • Clean, focused UX
  • Less overwhelming
  • Guides the user

Cons

  • More navigation
  • Can't see all at once

B. Single Scrollable Form

Everything on one long page — title, description, category, difficulty, time, ingredients, steps, photo, and dietary tags all visible by scrolling.

Pros

  • See everything at once
  • Simpler navigation
  • Faster for power users

Cons

  • Can feel overwhelming
  • Long scroll distance
